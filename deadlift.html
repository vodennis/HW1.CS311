The Deadlift

The deadlift is a lift that targets the posterior chain like the lower back,
glutes, hamstrings, and traps. It is one of the most
effective exercises for building overall strength and power.

  1. Stand with your feet around shoulder width under the barbell
  2. Grip the bar outside your legs
  3. Lower your hips and bend your knees until your shins close to or touching the bar
  4. Brace and keep your back straight and lift the bar up by driving through your heels
  5. Lockout using your hips until you are in an upright position then lower the bar back to the ground

Common Mistakes

  • Rounding your back during the lift
  • Not locking out fully at the top of the lift
