The Bench Press

The bench press is an exercise that targets the chest,
shoulders, and triceps. It is important for building upper body strength and is very common
in most lifting routines.

  1. Lay on the bench
  2. Grip the barbell with hands slightly wider than shoulder width
  3. Bring the barbell to your around your mid chest and try to keep your elbows in
  4. Push the barbell back up to your starting position until your arms are fully extended

Common Mistakes

  • Bouncing the bar off your chest
  • Flaring your elbows
