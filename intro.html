Introduction to Powerlifting

Powerlifting is a training style that revolves around the three main compounds: squat, bench, and deadlift.
Powerlifters try to maximize the amount of weight they can lift in all three of these compounds.
These three exercises are the foundation of strength training and are referred to as The Big Three.
