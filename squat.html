The Squat

The squat is a compound lift that trains the thighs, hips,
glutes, quadriceps, and hamstrings, as well as strengthening the overall
ligaments and tendons in the lower body.

  1. Line the barbell on your upper back
  2. Place your feet up around shoulder width apart
  3. Brace your core and bend at your knees and hips
  4. Lower yourself until your hips are in line with your knees
  5. Push through your heels to get back to your starting position

Common Mistakes

  • Not going deep enough in the squat
  • Letting the knees cave inward (bad for knees)
  • Rounding the lower back on the way up or down
